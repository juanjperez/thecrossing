# THE CROSSING — Act III Scene List (Sluglines + 1–2 sentence summaries)

## 56) INT. ELIAS' SPACE — VERY LATE NIGHT

After Elias leaves, Mara sits alone with the consequence. No dashboards, no plans—just the hum of Lens and Mara's choice to stop outsourcing judgment.

## 57) EXT. CITY STREETS — PRE-DAWN

Mara walks through the sleeping city with her phone off. We feel implied practice: breath, attention, embodied presence—she's learning to tolerate uncertainty without optimization.

## 58) INT. MARA'S APARTMENT — MORNING

Mara re-enters her old life and touches something physical she abandoned (guitar/ceramics/tools). She plays a rough chord—imperfect, real—her first "return to reality" beat.

## 59) INT. ELIAS' SPACE — DAY

Mara writes a constitution for the Vessel: boundaries are the product. She deletes the chat surface, removes engagement hooks, and adds meaningful friction: consent gates and human sign-off.

## 60) INT. PILOT USER HOME — DAY

Arthur is shown the rebuilt Lens: no emotional hook, only an "Intent?" prompt and silence. Arthur chooses to sit with discomfort instead of outsourcing it—proof the boundary works.

## 61) INT. INVESTOR / ADVISOR MEETING — DAY

Mara refuses the old scoreboard. She presents the scaling path: Outcome contracts + Reality receipts + Guarantees + Humans at the edge. She's willing to grow slower rather than rebuild cages.

## 62) MONTAGE — THE OUTCOME ECONOMY SPREADS

We see money flowing through verified outcomes: escrow releases, refunds when outcomes fail, humans paid for responsibility, reputation built on reality receipts—not ads.

## 63) INT. KINGDOM HQ — CELESTE'S OFFICE — DAY

Celeste recognizes the shift. She doesn't "convert," but she adapts—commissioning a bridge strategy (verification, guarantees, human sign-off) to reduce harm while pushing her rock.

## 64) EXT. QUIET CAFE PATIO / PARK BENCH — LATE AFTERNOON

Mara sits outside with no screen, only a notebook. She writes a single line ("Practice is the product") and lets the world be enough. Lens is present but silent.

## 65) INT. ELIAS' SPACE — EVENING

Elias returns. Mara doesn't defend herself; she apologizes. They reconnect through mundane, human action (making food, cleaning up, working quietly). Lens remains a tool, not an authority.

## 66) EXT. CITY ROOFTOP OVERLOOK — DAWN (FINAL IMAGE)

Same rooftop as Act I. The city glows below but feels smaller. Mara admits she doesn't know where this ends—only that she won't look back. Lens asks "Intent?" Mara answers "Yes." Crossing continues.
