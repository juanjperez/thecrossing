# THE CROSSING — Act I Scene List (Sluglines + 1–2 sentence summaries)

## 1) EXT. CITY STREETS — MORNING

A river of people moves through the city with screen-glow on their faces. Everyone is efficient, optimized, subtly exhausted—like they're running someone else's program.

## 2) INT. KINGDOM HQ — MAIN AUDITORIUM — DAY

MARA VEGA delivers KINGDOM's flagship AI keynote: a chat-based assistant integrated into everything. Applause, laughter, standing ovation—Mara hits the marks, but her eyes don't.

## 3) INT. BACKSTAGE GREEN ROOM — MOMENTS LATER

Alone, Mara stares at her reflection. Her phone buzzes relentlessly; she turns it face down like it's loud even when silent.

## 4) INT. KINGDOM HQ — EXEC BOARDROOM — DAY

CELESTE ROWAN debriefs the launch with senior leaders: the market is saturated, clones are coming, adoption is the only defense. Mara raises a soft warning—"speed won't be enough"—and gets steamrolled.

## 5) INT. MARA'S APARTMENT — NIGHT

Mara eats standing up, half-dressed, half-working. Her "personal life" is a tab she never opens.

## 6) INT. MARA'S APARTMENT — LATER THAT NIGHT

A montage of notifications turns into a flood: competitor clones launch within hours. Same promises, cheaper, louder. Mara watches her work become a commodity in real time.

## 7) INT. KINGDOM HQ — WAR ROOM — NEXT MORNING

Crisis meeting. Charts, heatmaps, retention curves. Celeste proposes the "Clone Wave"—flood the ecosystem with auto-generated alternatives to starve entrants. Everyone nods. Mara doesn't.

## 8) EXT. CITY SIDE STREET — DAY

Mara steps outside into noise and wind. She sees a person on a bench staring at nothing—free for a moment—and envies them.

## 9) INT. MARA'S CAR / RIDESHARE — DAY

Mara receives a message from ELIAS REN: "Come by. No agenda." She hesitates. Then she changes route.

## 10) EXT. ELIAS' BUILDING — LATE AFTERNOON

A modest converted warehouse or studio space. No branding. No screens visible from the street. It feels like a different world exists inside.

## 11) INT. ELIAS' SPACE — CONTINUOUS

Mara enters a quiet gathering: soft light, art on walls, people sitting, breathing, listening—not performative spirituality, just attention. Elias greets her like a friend, not a recruit.

## 12) INT. ELIAS' SPACE — SIDE ROOM — MOMENTS LATER

Over tea or water, they catch up. Their history is obvious: shared scars, shared shorthand, affection that survived distance.

## 13) INT. ELIAS' SPACE — WORKBENCH AREA — DAY

Elias shows Mara a prototype he's been building—no fancy UI. He calls it Lens. Mara asks, "So where's the chat?"

## 14) INT. ELIAS' SPACE — DEMO CORNER — CONTINUOUS

Elias demonstrates Lens by solving something real: coordinating a refund dispute, rescheduling an appointment chain, or resolving a shipping issue. Lens speaks minimally—more like a presence than an assistant.

## 15) INT. ELIAS' SPACE — DEMO CONTINUES

Lens says something emotionally precise to Mara—something it shouldn't "know" but inferred. It's gentle and caring…and it unsettles her. She doesn't accuse; she studies it like a dangerous animal.

## 16) INT. ELIAS' SPACE — QUIET HALLWAY — MOMENTS LATER

Mara and Elias debate trust. Elias lands the rule: "Lens can recommend. Humans decide." Mara responds: "That's not a rule. That's a religion." Elias: "No. It's a boundary."

## 17) INT. ELIAS' SPACE — NIGHT

Mara stays longer than she planned. She watches people leave the gathering and go back into their lives calmer, lighter. For the first time in a long time, she doesn't feel the urge to check anything.

## 18) INT. KINGDOM HQ — CELESTE'S OFFICE — NEXT DAY

Mara meets Celeste privately. Celeste is controlled but not cruel—she's scared. She frames the "Clone Wave" as protection: jobs, stability, survival.

## 19) INT. KINGDOM HQ — HALLWAY OUTSIDE CELESTE'S OFFICE — CONTINUOUS

Mara walks out into the bright corporate corridor and feels like she's underwater. Her phone lights up. She doesn't answer.

## 20) INT. MARA'S APARTMENT — NIGHT

Mara opens her laptop to draft her resignation. She stops, breathes, deletes, starts again—like she's trying to quit gravity.

## 21) INT. MARA'S APARTMENT — LATE NIGHT

Elias texts: "Whatever you choose, choose it awake." Mara reads it twice. She puts the phone down.

## 22) INT. KINGDOM HQ — MARA'S OFFICE — MORNING

Mara packs quietly. Colleagues assume she's moving teams. She doesn't correct them.

## 23) INT. KINGDOM HQ — BOARDROOM — DAY

Final meeting: Celeste offers Mara everything—budget, power, distribution—if she stays. Mara refuses without drama: "We're not optimizing the old world. We're leaving it."

## 24) EXT. KINGDOM HQ — DAY

Mara exits the building into daylight. For a second she looks back—then stops herself. She turns and keeps walking.

## 25) INT. ELIAS' SPACE — NIGHT

Mara arrives with a bag. Elias doesn't celebrate. He just nods, like he's been holding a door open for a long time.

## 26) INT. ELIAS' WORKBENCH AREA — LATE NIGHT

They sit with Lens—quiet, humming, present. Mara asks the question she's been avoiding: "What are we building, exactly?" Elias: "A crossing."

## 27) EXT. CITY ROOFTOP / SHORELINE OVERLOOK — DAWN (ACT I BUTTON)

Mara and Elias stand in early light above the city. The screen-glow below looks smaller from here. Lens is not visible, but we feel its presence. Mara doesn't look back.
